## Available Reports

FinTrack provides several reports for analyzing your finances:

| Report | Purpose |
|:-------|:--------|
| **Net Worth History** | Track wealth over time |
| **Monthly Report** | Drill down by account, category, or tag |
| **Income Statement** | Monthly income vs. expenses |
| **Balance Sheet** | Assets vs. liabilities |

---

## Net Worth History

Dedicated report tracking your complete financial picture:

### Metrics Tracked
- **Total Net Worth** - Assets minus liabilities
- **Cash** - Checking + Savings
- **Investments** - Portfolio value
- **Debt** - Credit cards and loans

### Features
- Monthly historical data table
- Chart visualization of trends
- Year filter (shows 12 months for past years)
- Current year shows months to date

---

## Monthly Report

Unified view for drilling into financial data:

### View Modes

1. **By Account** - See spending per account
2. **By Category** - Spending by category
3. **By Tag** - Cross-category tag analysis

### Flow Modes

- **Spending** - Excludes transfer transactions
- **Net Flow** - Includes all transactions

### Interactive Features

- Click any cell to view underlying transactions
- Color-coded cells (income green, expenses red)
- Monthly totals row

---

## Income Statement

Track income vs. expenses monthly:

- Income categories at top
- Expense categories below
- Net income calculation
- Click to drill down into transactions

---

## Balance Sheet

Assets and liabilities snapshot:

- **Assets** - All positive-balance accounts
- **Liabilities** - Credit cards and loans
- **Net Worth** - Total assets minus liabilities
- Historical comparison available

---

## Drilldowns

All reports support interactive drilldowns:

1. Click any value in a report
2. View the transactions that make up that value
3. Edit transactions directly from the drilldown
4. Changes reflect immediately in reports
