## Overview

The Dashboard is your financial command center, providing a high-level view of your net worth, income/expense flows, and quick access to common actions.

---

## Net Worth Summary

At the top of the dashboard, you'll see:

- **Total Net Worth** - Sum of all assets minus liabilities
- **Cash** - Checking and savings balances
- **Investments** - Portfolio value
- **Debt** - Credit card and loan balances

---

## Sankey Chart

The enhanced Sankey diagram visualizes your money flow:

- **Income sources** on the left (salary, investments, etc.)
- **Spending categories** on the right
- Flow width represents relative amounts
- Hover for exact values

---

## Quick Actions

Fast access buttons for common tasks:

| Action | Description |
|:-------|:------------|
| **Add Transaction** | Create a new income or expense |
| **Add Transfer** | Move money between accounts |
| **Add Trade** | Record a stock trade |
| **View Staging** | Review pending bank transactions |

The staging badge shows the count of pending items requiring attention.

---

## Recurring Transaction Alerts

When recurring transactions are overdue:

- Visual indicator appears on the dashboard
- Click to view details in a modal
- Shows days overdue and expected amounts
- Helps you catch missed bills or subscriptions

---

## Recent Activity

View your most recent transactions with:

- Date and description
- Amount (color-coded by type)
- Category
- Click any transaction to edit
